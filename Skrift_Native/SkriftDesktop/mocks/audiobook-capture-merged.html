Locked decisions

• Player fills the screen. The read-along (the real ReadAlongView: uniform 18 pt lines, current line lit + scaleEffect(1.08), neighbours 0.6 / far 0.32 opacity, past skTextFaint / upcoming skTextDim, edge fade) expands to fill all space below the header; scrubber → transport → Chapters·Bookmark → Capture this are pinned at the bottom. No dead space. Header/cover unchanged; the warm band is the per-book cover tint.

• Capture is ONE note-style screen. Order mirrors the memo note exactly: header (❝ + “Capture” + book·chapter·start→end) → significance (the real SignificanceCircles card: label + live value, 10 circles, amber refine-wall before circle 8, PASSING/USEFUL/SIGNIFICANT tier labels, flag-to-send sync line; warms + shows the flame “refine pass” at 0.8+) → build-your-quote body (the real TextCaptureView sentence rows: tap + to add a line, ✕ to drop an edge; plain text; filled plus/check/xmark.circle.fill discs) + attribution preview → Record your thoughts pinned at the bottom (the real record button, no “book stays paused” subtitle).

• Always records voice — single action, no quote-only save (revisit later if wanted). On tap: build the quote from the selection → create the memo → apply significance → open the recorder. After the ramble it auto-resumes the book and lands as the normal note — no separate preview/review step (the append transcribes in the background; MemoSaver.appendRecording is fire-and-forget).

• Audio mark-in/out arm retired. Text capture is the only flow — CaptureMomentView, the AudiobookCaptureStyle A/B seam (+ its Settings toggle), and the old capture sheet’s sentence-trim are removed. The merged screen works even on an un-chunked spot (it transcribes the ~90 s window live).
