Where should the audio player live?

Today the note owns one dense top bar — transport, Process, add, overflow, and both panel toggles all in a row. The idea on the table: drop the player to the bottom (like the phone) and give the top a calmer job. Three ways to cut it, drawn on the real three-panel layout.

The live question underneath: is a bottom player actually clearer, or just further from the transcript you're reading on a 13″ screen? C is the honest control so we can tell.
