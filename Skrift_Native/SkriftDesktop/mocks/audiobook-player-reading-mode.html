The decisions (the spec)

Navigation / IA — a tab bar (screen 1)

  • Notes · Library · (Highlights) · Settings as a bottom tab bar. The Library stops being a dismissible .sheet → pull-to-refresh works, and it reads as co-equal with Notes (the vision = both first-class). Highlights is shown dimmed = the Phase-6 slot (book quotes become notes); ships then, not now.
  • Delete needs a confirm — one swipe currently nukes a book. Confirm sheet spells out local-only vs also-stops-syncing.

The player — “reading mode” (screens 2 & 3)

  • Compressed header (Henry): “NOW PLAYING” is gone; cover-chip + title + author + chapter live in one slim bar. In reading mode it shrinks to a one-line “Sapiens · ch 4 / −21:13”.
  • Accent the now, don’t hide the rest: the whole page is readable; the current line is brightest and the current word gets the accent chip; past/ahead text stays legible (not blacked out). You can free-scroll to read ahead; a “Back to playing” pill returns you when you’ve wandered.
  • Floating play button (screen 3) matches the memo screen’s floating play — always under your thumb. Skip ±15/30 flank it when controls are up (screen 2); in reading mode just the float shows.
  • “Capture this” → “Add note” — demoted from a big pill to a small accent chip in the utility row. The capture flow is unchanged; it just stops dominating.
  • Speed + Sleep + Bookmark + Aa sit in a quiet utility row; everything but the page recedes while reading.

Text settings — the “Aa” button (screen 4)

  • Size stepper (small-A / big-A) + line-spacing (Tight/Cozy/Loose) in v1 — the two that actually make long reading comfortable. Light/Sepia/Dark reading theme is the fast-follow. Persists app-wide.

Bookmarks — book-like, and the model untangled (screens 2, 3, 5)

  • Add = the Bookmark button in the utility row (drops/removes a mark at your spot). Browse = the sheet (Chapters | Bookmarks), which ONLY jumps — fixing today’s confusing “Bookmarks tab adds, Chapters tab navigates”.
  • Margin ribbon (screen 3): a bookmarked spot shows an accent ribbon in the left margin that scrolls with the text — so you can scroll and see “this bit is saved”, just like a dog-eared page. A bookmark is really a timestamp → the ribbon maps to the nearest text when the book’s transcribed, and the list always works regardless.

Open questions for you 👇

Q1 — Tabs: commit to the bottom tab bar (Notes · Library · Highlights · Settings)? Or the cheaper interim (Library becomes a full-screen page instead of a sheet, no tabs yet)?
Q2 — “Importance” relabel: significance is being de-Mac’d into a per-note flag. Best word: Importance, Pin, or Highlight? (Needed for the Phase-3 work that rides alongside this.)
Q3 — Add-note placement: small chip in the utility row (shown), or a tiny floating button near the play control?
Q4 — Reading mode trigger: auto-recede after a few seconds of no touch (Kindle-style), or only when you scroll the text?
