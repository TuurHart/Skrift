  • ◆ green markers (#m1), verdicts in: ◆1 tags-under-context YES · ◆2 compact significance — re-confirm (Tuur's hesitation was a feared clash with an "embedder significance"; no such number exists — circles are always the user's rating — so the objection dissolves, pending his OK) · ◆3 chip icons YES.
