Audiobook quote-capture — Skrift IS the player (modeled on Bound)

Per the locked design: Files/iCloud import, library, per-book resume, speed, sleep, chapters — and ONE new verb: Capture. It's retroactive — pauses the book and proposes [now −30 s → now]; a ~15 s-grain micro-scrubber adjusts IN/OUT with audio grains under your finger, both edges snapping outward to whole sentences. The span is transcribed on-demand (never the whole book) into one memo per capture: italic quote + attribution + your voice ramble; the span's audio IS the memo's audio. Everything is clickable — use the mode bar, or ride the flow: Library row → player → Capture → confirm → sheet → save.

The capsule exists ONLY while a book session is active — no session, no bar, and the FAB sits in its usual spot. The capture row wears the book glyph + the quote as its (italic) title. The mini-player yields on memo detail (book keeps playing underneath); the FAB auto-pauses the book and resumes it after save. ˄ expands, ❝ captures from right here.

Flagged choices, easy to flip: (1) Circles on the capture sheet — included (compact, no tier labels), because significance is the flag-to-send gate and a capture you don't rate would need a second visit before it syncs — same argument as the capture-items sheet; if the sheet must be leaner, delete the card and rate in memo detail like any memo. (2) Micro-scrubber treatment — a zoomed-window strip: chapter bar + zoom cone on top, waveform with sentence-boundary ticks, IN/OUT handles, a grain-play chip that lights while scrubbing. The cheaper alternative is two stacked plain sliders (coarse + fine) with no waveform/cone — drop .zoomctx + .wave and keep the handles on a bare track. (3) Mini-player contents/order — cover → ⟲15 → ▶ → 15⟳ → ❝ Capture (labeled, accent tint) → ˄; Capture gets the only label because it's the headline verb, and the cover carries identity so no title text fits the capsule. Alternative: title marquee instead of the skip buttons, or Capture as a bare ❝ glyph. (4) Capture in the full player — a full-width accent pill UNDER the transport (thumb zone, unmissable, transport stays a clean Bound triple). Alternative: a 4th roundel inside the transport row, or a toolbar ❝ top-right. (5) No mini-player on the Library screen — the Playing row already shows the session there; add the capsule to state 1 if switching-while-browsing matters more than a clean list. Also locked-but-shown: the proposed span is fixed at 30 s (the micro-scrubber is the adjustment, not a setting); sentence ticks come from the span transcription's word timings.
