The Fading shelf — where untouched notes go to be forgotten (visibly)

Design locked 2026-07-17: untouched notes (no dots / edits / title / tags / lock / reminder / annotation / backlink) leave the main surfaces after 30 days, sit HERE 30 more, then flow into Recently Deleted (14d) and out. Never a tab — a shelf: one row with a count, one click away, one-tap rescue. Doing nothing IS the cleanup.
