Build notes

• Read-along text reads from the wave-2 BookTranscript sidecar for the playhead window; the current line uses the existing karaoke highlight (Karaoke.activeWordIndex on the word-timings) lifted from memo detail. Past lines fade, a hint of upcoming text trails. No on/off toggle in v1.

• Nudge shows when the spot isn't chunked yet → routes to the existing TranscribeBookView. This is the growth loop: the player sells the transcribe feature.

• Capture stays the bottom hero, label "Capture this" (text shown) / "Capture a quote" (nudge state). Same QuoteCaptureFlowView seam — unchanged.

• Speed (left) + sleep (right) flank the transport; both already wired (AudiobookSession.setRate / setSleep).

• Chapters promoted out of the ⋯ menu into the slim row → opens the TOC sheet (already have chapters + displayChapterTitles + seek).

• Bookmark = NET-NEW, lightweight: tap drops a marker (global position + chapter label + timestamp), haptic + toast. List lives in the sheet under a Bookmarks tab (jump on tap, swipe-delete). NO audio/text — Capture remains the rich save. Persist next to the library (small JSON, per book).

• Out of scope: AirPlay/output button (Control Center + lock screen cover it), skip-silence, EQ/voice-boost.
