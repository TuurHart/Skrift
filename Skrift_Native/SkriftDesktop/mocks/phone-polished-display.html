Build notes — locked decisions (2026-06-26)

  • One editable body, starting from the polish — no toggle. When a MemoEnhancement.hasContent exists, the body shows the Mac's copy-edit (re-linked names via the shared Sanitiser), editable in place. No polish → the raw transcript as today. (Mirrors the Mac: polish gives you a cleaner draft, you tweak it.)
  • Your edits are the source of truth + sync. Editing writes the MemoEnhancement.copyedit (stamped enhancedByDeviceID = this phone, enhancedAt = now), which CloudKit syncs to the Mac + iPad as the new truth. The RAW transcript stays the contract input (uploaded to the Mac, untouched). No clobber — the Mac only processes a memo whose enhanceStatus != .done; once polished it's never auto-re-polished (verified in ProcessingCoordinator.needsProcessing). No drift — raw = input, polished(+edits) = output; nothing re-derives polished from raw once it exists.
  • Title chooser (the parked phone problem) = a compact bottom sheet, not two cards. The ✦ button opens a list: Suggested (the Mac's title, ✦) · From the recording (transcript first line) · your own (edit). A check marks the active one. Picking just sets memo.title.
  • Summary card (✦) above the body when the Mac wrote one; hidden otherwise.
  • Provenance: a quiet "✦ Polished on your Mac · <ago>" caption.
  • Karaoke = PROPORTIONAL on the polished body (v1). Word timings are pinned to the RAW words; polishing rewrites them, so the per-word 1:1 mapping breaks. Like the Mac, the highlight tracks progress proportionally (follow-along) rather than word-exact. Audio + raw timings are kept intact. Fast-follow: re-align the polished words to the raw timestamps (token diff: unchanged words inherit their exact time, new ones interpolate) → near word-exact karaoke + "scrub to a word and fix it by ear" on the polished text.
  • Name tiers (task 1) apply to this body too — names render accent + are tappable to resolve (the resolution is a per-note pick; it never edits the words, so it's safe in either text).
  • No on-device polish here — that's the separate Phase-4 spike. This screen only surfaces a paired Mac's result.
