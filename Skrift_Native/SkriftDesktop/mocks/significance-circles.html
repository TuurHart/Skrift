Flagged choices, easy to flip: circle size 13px / gap 7px on the Mac (≈200px total) vs 19px / 9px touch targets on the phone — both one constant. The refine wall is shown three ways at once (always-visible amber hairline before circle 8 + warm-tinted fills on circles 8–10 when lit + the flame “refine pass” tag after the row); any of the three can be dropped if it's too much. Tier label lives in two places: the live “0.5 · Useful” top-right (same spot as today's slider value) and faint group labels under the circle clusters (1–3 / 4–6 / 7–10) that light up with the rating — keep one if both is noisy.
