Build notes — locked decisions (sign-off makes these the spec)

  • Tab IA: Journal takes the reserved "Highlights (soon)" slot → Notes · Library · Journal · Settings. P6 (Highlights feed, Daily Review, quote cards) later lands inside Journal as sections. ← the one IA decision to confirm at sign-off.
  • Looking back = spaced lookbacks (1/3/6/12 months) + a literal On-this-day card once prior-year history exists. Slots with nothing to show are hidden. Card picks the highest-importance note of the window.
  • Then vs Now (fast-follow): a future Looking-back card variant that pairs an old note with a recent same-cluster note ("a year ago you thought… last week you said…"). Juxtaposition only, no interpretation.
  • Thread = related() above floor, date-ascending; header = first mention's title + date. Entry points: Related card CTA + note ••• menu. No thread list on Journal home in v1.
  • Search: Matches (existing substring, unchanged) above Related (semantic; shows when query ≥2 words or exact hits <3). Chips prefilter person/place/month/kind. Voice search = fast-follow (mic dimmed).
  • No numbers: similarity scores never render. Relevance = presence + order.
  • Corpus: Skrift memos only (the source of truth); the Obsidian vault is never indexed in v1 (later opt-in "vault lens"). Unrated notes are provisional — auto-prune (i2) may remove them after the review flow; the index sweep deletes orphaned embeddings.
  • Calendar + map + lookbacks are ML-free (pure metadata) and ship even if the embedding spike fails; Related/Thread/semantic-Related-section are the embedding-gated half.
  • Privacy: everything on-device (NL framework embeddings, local index, MapKit). Nothing leaves the phone.
