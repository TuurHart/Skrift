Pricing lands at three tiers: a free capture-only plan, a $6/mo personal plan gating sync and search, and a $14 team plan. The interesting move is what's NOT gated — transcription is unlimited on free, betting the lock-in happens at the library layer, not the capture layer.

Retention math from their public numbers: 38% of trial users convert when they import an existing archive in week one, versus 9% who start empty. Import is the whole funnel.

Expanded: the text flows in the note's own type, slightly dimmed — readable, selectable, quotable, but visually subordinate to the user's words. Long documents preview the first stretch + "Show all N pages" (full text in a pushed reader screen, not an endless scroll inside the note).
